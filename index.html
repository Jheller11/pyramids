Move the Pyramid!

How to Play:
1. Select the top block from any column by clicking on that column.
2. Select a column to place the block in.

To win, move blocks from the first column to the last column given the following rules:
1. You may only move one block at a time.
2. You may not place a larger block on top of a smaller block.
